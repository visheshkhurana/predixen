High
BUG-H1: /data page title still says "RunwayAI - Startup Financial Simulator"
The main pages were rebranded, but the /data route still has the old title in the browser tab. This is a simple metadata fix.
BUG-H2: AI Copilot gives generic template responses, not data-driven answers
When asked "What if we hire 5 engineers next quarter?" the copilot responds with the same canned template every time: "Reduce burn by 20%, Increase prices by 10%, Freeze hiring for 6 months, Delay fundraise by 3 months." It never uses the company's actual financials ($95K MRR, $1.43M cash, $89K burn) to give a specific answer like "Adding 5 engineers at ~$15K/mo each would increase burn to ~$164K/mo and reduce runway from 16.1 to ~8.7 months." This makes the copilot feel like a demo, not a real tool.
BUG-H3: Revenue Growth shows 0.0% instead of N/A or a calculated value
On multiple pages, Revenue Growth is displayed as "+0%" or "0.0%." If there's only one month of data, this should display "N/A" or "Insufficient data" rather than a misleading zero.
BUG-H4: Headcount shows $0K/employee on Data Input
The Data Input page shows Headcount: 10 with "$0K/emp" — the per-employee cost calculation is broken.
Medium
BUG-M1: Strategic Builder — strategy cards are not selectable
On the /scenarios Strategic Builder, clicking any strategy card (Conservative Cost Cutting, Deep Cost Restructuring, Price Increase Strategy) does nothing. The footer says "Select a strategy to customize" but the cards have no click interaction. The "Run Simulation" button stays inactive. Users must use the Classic Wizard instead, which defeats the purpose of the Strategic Builder UX.
BUG-M2: Monte Carlo simulation has near-zero variance
The Baseline scenario's 1,000-run Monte Carlo simulation produced P10 = 13.0 mo, P50 = 14.0 mo, P90 = 14.0 mo — a range of only 1 month. Real Monte Carlo simulations should show meaningful variance (e.g., ±3–6 months). The random variance parameters may be too low, making the simulation feel deterministic.
BUG-M3: Metric Catalog "Initialize System Metrics" appears non-functional
Clicking the "Initialize System Metrics" button on /metrics doesn't produce visible feedback — no loading spinner, no success message, no metrics appear.
