Predixen ‚Äî Founder/CXO QA Bug Report (Demo)
Date: 2026-01-31
Tester mindset: Founder/CXO using product to make real decisions; exploratory QA across core modules.

=== Executive Summary ===
Predixen has strong surface area (Truth Scan, Simulation visuals, Templates catalog), but currently fails the #1 requirement for a decision product: trust in numbers. Multiple modules disagree on cash/runway/survival and even labeling (net burn). Until cross-module consistency + provenance is fixed, a founder cannot safely use outputs to make $-impacting decisions.

=== Environment / Setup ===
- Site: https://predixen.app
- Mode: Try Demo Simulation
- Sample company created: TechFlow AI
- Sample baseline used: Cash on Hand 500,000; Monthly Revenue 50,000; Total Monthly Expenses ~80,000; Growth 10% (values observed in UI)

=== Key Findings (P0/P1) ===

P0 ‚Äî Trust/Consistency Breakers
1) Overview shows Cash on Hand = $0 and Runway = 0.0 mo while Truth Scan and Simulation show Cash = $500K
   - Impact: destroys trust; dashboards become unusable for exec decision-making.

2) Simulation Results: Net Burn is labeled ‚ÄúCash positive‚Äù when Net Burn is shown as a positive burn figure (e.g., $30K)
   - Impact: CFO/finance users will consider model unreliable.

3) Copilot contradicts displayed simulation KPIs
   - UI sidebar shows Survival 18m: 100% (baseline), while Copilot response cited baseline survival (18m) as ~50%.
   - Impact: ‚ÄúAI advisor‚Äù becomes a liability without citations and alignment to scenario artifacts.

P1 ‚Äî Core Feature Breakage / Misleading States
4) Alerts page says ‚ÄúData Required for Monitoring‚Äù even after data saved + Truth Scan run + Simulation run
   - Also shows alert counts but ‚ÄúOverall Status: Unknown‚Äù.

5) Alerts page CTAs link to incorrect routes
   - Observed: /data-input and /truth-scan
   - Expected: /data and /truth (or redirects).

6) Templates: ‚ÄúApply Template‚Äù does not create a new saved scenario
   - After applying Pricing Increase, Saved scenarios still only shows Baseline.
   - Either create scenario automatically OR rename CTA to ‚ÄúOpen in Builder‚Äù and require explicit save.

7) Fundraising: ‚ÄúCreate Cap Table‚Äù does not complete
   - Dialog remains open; no success toast; no cap table appears.
