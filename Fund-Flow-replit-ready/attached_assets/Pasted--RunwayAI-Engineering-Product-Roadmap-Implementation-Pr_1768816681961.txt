# RunwayAI - Engineering & Product Roadmap Implementation Prompt

## Core Product Goals
- Build predictive, founder-focused decision support
- Enable data-driven scenario simulation and actionable insights
- Provide real-time monitoring with alerts
- Support collaboration and investor readiness

## Phase 1 — Data & Analytics Foundation

### 1. Expand Data Integrations
- Add new connectors:
  * Stripe (revenue, payments)
  * HubSpot / Salesforce (CRM)
  * Chargebee / Recurly (subscription data)
- Implement scheduled and real-time sync
- Add UI element showing “Last data sync timestamp”
- Validate incoming data shapes and surface issues to users

### 2. Product & Usage Analytics
- Instrument events (login, scenario create, run, export, engagement)
- Track feature adoption and onboarding funnels
- Build dashboards with:
  * Active users
  * Feature usage heatmap
  * Churn & retention metrics
  * User cohorts for power indicator tracking
This aligns with product analytics frameworks and improves feature prioritization over gut reactions. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

### 3. Enrich Simulations with Predictive Models
- Incorporate Monte Carlo and robust decision-making logic to show ranges of outcomes not single points
- Apply predictive analytics to revenue & churn forecasting
- Store scenario outputs for comparison and trend analysis

## Phase 2 — Core Product Features & UX

### 4. Scenario Builder Improvements
- Enable custom scenario creation (define events + probability distributions)
- Save user-generated templates
- Show multiple runs (distribution view) and comparisons
- Add scenario tagging & versioning

### 5. Benchmarking & Contextual Guidance
- Allow users to select benchmark cohorts (stage, industry)
- Surface industry metrics beside user data
- Define actionable insights (not just dashboards)

### 6. Decision Insights
- Compute and display quantitative impact for each recommendation
- Provide rationale text like:
  * “Runway projected below 12 months by Q3; increasing prices extends runway by X months”
  * “Cutting CAC by Y% improves net revenue by Z%”
- Show side-by-side comparison of top recommendations with simulation results

## Phase 3 — Collaboration & External Output

### 7. Team Collaboration
- Add workspace sharing, roles & permissions
- Commenting & annotation on scenarios and decisions
- Exportable reports (PDF, spreadsheet) tailored to founders and investors

### 8. Alerts & Monitoring
- Provide preset alert thresholds (runway < 12mo, negative growth, churn spike)
- Add Slack / email notifications
- Provide alert configuration UI

## Phase 4 — AI Copilot & Intelligence

### 9. AI Copilot Enhancements
- Central AI gateway to manage requests securely
- Copilot answers grounded in actual user data
- Suggested next-step questions & coaching
- Provide contextual suggestions and learning explanations for metrics and actions

From best practices: AI should be implemented intentionally, aligned with business goals and used to provide meaningful outcomes. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

### 10. Smart Suggestions & Augmented AI
- Augmented AI that pairs system suggestions with human context for decisions
- Autocomplete for query prompts and scenario templates
- Use a recommendation system approach internally to suggest template scenarios based on user data

## Phase 5 — Product Strategy & Market Adoption

### 11. Product Roadmap & Iterative Prioritization
- Define meaningful business KPIs (MRR growth, churn, runway horizon)
- Prioritize features based on impact and effort (MVP -> growth -> scale)
- Maintain roadmap as a living, reviewable timeline document aligned to company goals. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

### 12. Documentation & Learning
- In-product tooltips and glossary descriptions
- Tutorials for simulation creation and interpretation
- Benchmark playbooks for common founder questions

### 13. Go-to-Market & Growth
- Templates for investor decks and fundraising simulations
- Partner with accelerators & VC firms as official tool
- Community playbooks and shared scenario templates

---

## Technical Notes
- Maintain API first architecture for integrations and extensibility  
- Use modular microservices for analytics, simulation engine, AI gateway  
- Backend: event store for scenarios and metrics  
- Frontend: user dashboards + collaborative workspaces  
- Security: SOC2 readiness and strong access controls

---

## Success Metrics
Define measurable goals:
- % of users syncing external data
- Scenario adoption rates
- User engagement & retention
- Accuracy of predictive outcomes
- Alerts opened and acted upon
- Conversion lift shown in investor simulations
